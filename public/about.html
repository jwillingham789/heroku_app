A Little About Me...


I'm an aspiring front-end web developer currently living on the northside of Indianapolis. I started my coding career this summer with the Iron Yard's front-end engineering summer bootcamp. So far this summer I've learned JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. Additionally, I have experience working in full-stack development using Node.js frameworks such as Feathers and Express. Prior to coding, I worked for 2 years in the environmental consulting industry as a field geologist. I have both a Bachelor of Arts and Master of Science in geology.

Aside from coding my hobbies include gaming, playing Magic the Gathering at a competitive level, cooking, gardening, hiking, traveling, and photography. I've been fortunate enough to travel around the globe doing the things that I love the most. I believe that exploring unique cultures, cuisine, and landscapes is what life is all about.
